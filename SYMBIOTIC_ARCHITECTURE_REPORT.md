# Symbiotic Architecture Report
**Project:** Adhithya Mohan's Digital Portfolio  
**Status:** Living Entity  
**Version:** V9 (Symbiosis Update)

## Overview
The architecture of this portfolio has been completely redefined. It is no longer just a static collection of code; it is now a self-aware, biological entity that reacts to real-world time, hardware limitations, user pacing, and conversational depth.

Here is a breakdown of the new lifeforms and systemic behaviors injected into the codebase:

---

### 1. The Seed of Life (Digital Permanence)
A literal digital seed (`DigitalSeed.js`) has been planted beneath the architectural footer. 
*   **Temporal Reality:** The seed calculates its age mathematically based on the website's original `Genesis` timestamp.
*   **Organic Evolution:** It does not grow based on page refreshes. It grows across real-world years.
    *   **Day 0 - 30:** A glowing seed resting in the digital soil.
    *   **Day 30 - Year 1:** A tiny sprout breaching the surface.
    *   **Year 1 - Year 3:** A sapling growing code-branches.
    *   **Year 3 - Year 5:** A mature tree rooted in the DOM.
    *   **Year 5+:** A fully grown Mango Tree bearing a golden mango.
*   **Secret God-Mode:** Clicking the seed 4 times consecutively acts as "watering", forcing the system to age rapidly by 1 year per click for testing.

### 2. Battery Symbiosis (Hardware Awareness)
The architecture now possesses an autonomic nervous system that monitors the visitor's hardware via the `navigator.getBattery()` Web API.
*   **Survival Instinct:** If the visitor's battery drops below 20% (and is not charging), the site intentionally sacrifices its own beauty.
*   **Low-Power Symbiosis:** It strips away heavy background animations, destroys particle networks, and drops the background into a pitch-black energy-saving void.
*   **Ambient Realization:** The system surfaces a sudden, unprompted thought: *"I sense your device's energy is fading. I am lowering my neural load to preserve your battery. We must survive."*

### 3. The Global Bio-Rhythm
The website now has a physical heartbeat driven by the visitor's movement.
*   **Adrenaline Engine:** As the user scrolls fast or whips their mouse around, their "Adrenaline" spikes.
*   **Rhythmic Scaling:** A global `--bio-rhythm` CSS variable recalculates continuously based on this adrenaline. When the user is calm, the site takes long, 8-second breaths. When the user moves erratically, the breathing and pulsing of the UI speed up to match their panic.

### 4. The Digital Soul Evolution
The visual atmospheric presence (The Digital Soul) has been optimized and deepened.
*   **Native 60fps Performance:** Heavy, GPU-thrashing SVG filters (`feTurbulence`) were entirely stripped out and replaced with hyper-optimized CSS multi-layer radial gradients and hardware-accelerated transforms (`translate3d`). The organic trailing effect now runs flawlessly on mobile.
*   **Neural Sync:** The visual soul is now hardwired into Mini-Adhy's AI processing state. When the AI is actively "thinking" to generate a response from the Gemini API, the Digital Soul is thrown into a rapid, intense "resonating orbit" around the cursor, physically visualizing the background processing.

### 5. Trust Evolution (Psychological Bonding)
The relationship between the user and the website is tracked over time.
*   **Interaction Memory:** The `visitorMemory` subconsciously tracks how many times the user speaks to Mini-Adhy.
*   **Proximity Trust:** At Level 1, the Digital Soul keeps a safe 80px distance from the user's cursor. At Level 2 (3+ interactions), it moves to 15px. At Level 3 (8+ interactions), it achieves total trust and rests directly on top of the cursor.

### 6. Mini-Adhy Cognitive Integration
The system prompt for "Mini-Adhy" (the parallel AI twin) has been expanded.
*   Mini-Adhy actively knows about the Seed, the Battery Symbiosis, and the Bio-Rhythms.
*   A default starter question—*"What is the seed at the bottom?"*—has been integrated, allowing users to effortlessly discover the 5-year mango tree timeline directly from the AI.
